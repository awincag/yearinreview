Holy Ship Miami

Well, not really sure what to say for this one. Best week of my life? An absolutely epic week in Miami and the Bahamas. Mike had gone on Holy Ship the year prior and was raving about it all year until tickets came out. It was natural that we ended up pulling the trigger.

The flight on the way over was officially the 1st Annual, Unofficial Eastward Bound Plane Crawl. Mike and I weren't able to grab any direct flights.... or connecting flights. We ended up having 3 layovers on an overnight flight. We decided on the way that we would hit up as many airport bars as we could. And we did. And it was great! But what was even better, when we landed (we were sober by this point, don't worry), we had a Red, 2015 Jaguar F-Type S (my favorite car of all time), waiting for us. We drove that baby 450 miles in less than 24 hours, pretty much tearing up all of downtown Miami. We started in a huge mansion on Palm Island with our 20 closest friends. On the way to the ship, Mike and I were wearing Hawaiian shirts, in the Jag (with the top down), on the way to Holy Ship, blasting Panama (currently the song of year) and drifted coming out of the Island == Peak moment in life. Truly beautiful.

The ship itself was incredible. There was unlimited pizza. Literally unlimited pizza. How many places offer unlimited pizza?!?! All of our favorite DJ's were playing from 3pm to 6am everyday. We had an insane beach party which literally looked like it was out of a movie. There were men on the boat running around with lists of champagne. You'd point at the champagne, they'd go run and bring it back to you!! IT WAS CRAZY. At one point, we had literally partied / been up for 30 hours. Watching the sunrise over the Atlantic Ocean to Destructo's Sunrise Sermon at the top of the boat with champagne and my best friends was emotional. The whole trip was with Bestie, Bae and Babe. So yeah, best week of my life.

Mark Martin
