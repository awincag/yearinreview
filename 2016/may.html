May
Romper Room San Francisco

Rented out a bar to listen to daft punk! My camera got soaked from some idiot (me) who spilled Jack and Coke all over it. Didn't get as many pictures as I had hoped. Still a great time partying with bestie and babe.

Pure Beauty Pacifica

Did you know that there's a Taco Bell ON THE OCEAN BEACH? Yeah we found it, it's in pacifica. We went there. It was beautiful. Literally can walk up to the window to order FORM THE BEACH. I can't say enough good things about this place. It literally blew my mind.

Memorial Day Lake Tahoe

Mike had suggested a Tahoe trip for Memorial day. Beautiful Idea. Don't really need a reason to go up, Tahoe is always killer.

Nicole had flown in from NYC on Thursday night and we drove up to Tahoe Friday midmorning. On the way up, we stopped at Sol Food, arguably the greatest food ever created. We got up in the afternoon and met up with the rest of the gang, Mike, Hannah, Dalen, Hailey, Leah, Don and Leslie. We all started to make our way up to Tallac. Mike, Dalen and I tried making past the snow bowl. We didn't get very far before we just slide back down the mountain. Then we broke the boat. With a broken boat, we decided to play thunderstuck and beer pong and got absolutely hammered. I got sunburnt (I literally had to wear jeans the next time we went on the boat). On the last day, there was no one on the lake and a working boat, we drove out to emerald bay for quite possibly one of the prettiest boat rides I've ever been on. It was an amazing weekend.
