April
Around the World Bedrock, Santa Clara

Bedrock had their annual Around the world party. I didn't really dress up but the photos came out dope! Had a few people doing some flow arts and got some great shots of that too. First time I went to a party with a flash. It worked out great.

Kyra's Birthday San Francisco

Kyra is awesome. It's only fitting that we had an awesome birthday for her. Tyler had rented out a bus that took us all over the city. We went around to the Golden Gate Bridge, Treasure Island, 16th Steps in the Sunset, Golden Gate park and some breweries in the mission. Everyone got absolutely hammered. It was fantastic.

Pizzachella Palm Springs

4th year going to Coachella. This year, Gypsy hooked it up with a condo not too far away from the festival. We decided not to camp for the first time in two years and it was great cos 1) we got to sleep 2) we got to sleep 3) we got to shower.

Like the previous years, I brought in my camera. However, this year, my lens was a bit bigger than I had in the past. I got a bit nervous with the lens at first so I didn't bring it in the first half of the first day :(. The second day I was able to sneak my camera in. Lambert and I somehow made it in the Artist pit at the mainstage where we saw Ice Cube, Disclosure, and Guns' N Roses up close and personal. While we were in the pit, a security guard saw my camera and took it away thinking that I was paparazzi. After an hour of deliberation and convincing, the security guard gave me my camera back and decided not to kick me out and ban me. Thus, I did not bring in my camera in on the third day and did not get that many pictures :(

On a positive note, this year was epic. Lambert bought a ticket a week before the festival. Emily bought a ticket 2 days before the festival (I had to pick up the bracelet that Friday of the festival). Nicole had flown in all the way from the East Coast. Spicy Pie pizza was delicious, as always. The sets by m83, Chvrches, Grimes, Soulclap, James Bay, Chainsmokers, Guns N roses, Flume, Jack U and so many others were freaking awesome. Probably one of my favorite Coachella's to date. Hopefully will be going back next year.
