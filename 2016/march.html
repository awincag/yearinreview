March
Brewfest San Francisco

The annual SF Brefwest. This was my 6th time going. Went with some good ole Cal Poly Friends. We got hamms as per usual.

Side note: Jenna got Einstok beer and said ""hey this beer is from iceland."" I responded (without knowing that she was planning on going to Iceland too), ""I'm going to Iceland with my best friend Mike!"" It was at that moment that we decided to adventure to Iceland later this year. Had it not been for this moment, not sure if we would've gotten the 5 of us together to go to Iceland.

Alannah's Birthday Petulluma & Tomales Bay

For Alannah's birthday, Brian gathered the troops into a big ass RV and drove us up to Lagunitas for a pink themed birthday. Unfortunately, Lagunitas was too busy so we weren't able to hang out there, but we did make it across the street to another really dope brewery. After we were finished getting sufficiently hammered, we headed up to Tomales bay to camp and eat oysters. Everyone in the camp new a bit of flow arts so they took out the fire poi's and hoops and I did my first fire shoot. We got rained on, but it didn't make it any less cool.

Tony's Birthday San Francisco

Ah yes, the Tones! Tony's birthday this year consisted of 3 times and a ridiculous scavenger hunt list. Emily painted each team's face a different color, Tony gave us a task list and set us on our way. There was items ranging from make out with a stranger, take off a sock with your teeth, pump someone elses gass, deliver pizza to a park, make a pyramid with a corgi on top. There were some other items that I won't be repeating, but needless to say, things got weird and it was epic. Wrapped up the beautiful day with a nice picnic at some random park. Delicious.
