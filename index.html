November

Turkeys Can Fly

I hate flying a lot. Like a lot. It took me a very long time before I could fly sober. Luckily for me, Mike had gotten his flying license and wanted to take me up flying in a tiny plane with a tiny parachute. I somehow was convinced that it woudl be a good idea. I was wrong, it was absolutely fantastic. We flew around the bay area at about 1500 ft, which seems high but in fact feels very, very close to the ground. Every light bump feeels like the plane is going to crash, but Mike is actually pretty decent, so we didn't crash. A little poo came out.

If you've never flown in a prop plane, I highly recommend it.
